The migration and upgrade should be complete within 24 hours. Your existing site will be in a read-only state during the migration and upgrade process, and will redirect to your new site when finished.



What to expect before, during, and after migration:

  • Check-in documents – Any currently checked-out version of documents when migration starts will not be migrated to your new site.
  • Save an offline copy as needed – Your site will be in a read-only state and documents can’t be saved to the site during the migration window. If you access a read-only copy, make sure to save an offline copy locally.
  • A note about customizations – All site themes will be reset to the default SharePoint Online 2013 theme. Check out the SharePoint Online 101 guide for more information about giving your site a new 2013 look.
